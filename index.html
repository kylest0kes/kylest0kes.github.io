Eat The Burger

Eat The Burger is a app that lets users input the names of burgers they'd like to eat. Whenever a burger's name is submitted, it will display the burger in the To Devour area. Each burger in the waiting area also has a Devour button. When the user clicks it, the burger will move to the Devoured area.
